1

Upload Your Drawing

Start by uploading any drawing, sketch, or character illustration. Our AI automatically removes the background to isolate your character.

Supported Formats
  • PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP
  • Up to 10MB file size
  • Transparent backgrounds work best
Tips for Best Results
  • Use high contrast between character and background
  • Draw characters with clear, visible limbs
  • Front-facing or T-pose works best for detection
2

AI Pose Detection

Our AI analyzes your drawing and automatically detects body landmarks to create a skeleton rig. This rig is what enables smooth animations.

What Gets Detected
  • Head, neck, and torso positions
  • Shoulder, elbow, and wrist joints
  • Hip, knee, and ankle positions
  • Optional: finger and face landmarks

Manual Adjustments: If detection isn't perfect, you can manually adjust joint positions using our visual editor.

3

Choose Your Animation

Select from our library of motion presets or describe the animation you want and let AI generate it for you.

Motion Preset Library
  • Walk - Natural walking
  • Run - Running animation
  • Wave - Friendly wave
  • Dance - Fun moves
  • Jump - Jumping motion
  • Idle - Subtle breathing

AI Motion: Describe any motion in plain text like "waving both hands excitedly" and our AI generates custom keyframes.

4

Export & Share

Preview your animation and export it in your preferred format. Download and share directly to social media.

Export Formats
  • GIF - Perfect for social media and messaging
  • MP4 - High quality video with audio support
  • WebM - Smaller file size, web-optimized
  • PNG Sequence - For advanced editing software
Quality Options
  • Resolution: up to 4K (3840x2160)
  • Frame rate: 24, 30, or 60 FPS
