Tips for Best Results

Get the most out of your animated art

Clear Character Pose

Characters with visible limbs work best. Avoid heavy overlapping.

Good Contrast

Character should stand out from the background clearly.

Sufficient Resolution

At least 500px on the shortest side. Higher is better.

Front or 3/4 View

These views animate most naturally. Side views work too.
