General

Animate a Drawing is an AI-powered platform that brings your drawings to life. Upload any illustration, sketch, or character art, and our AI automatically detects the pose, creates a rig, and applies motion to create animations you can export as GIFs or videos.

Any drawing with a clear character outline works well. Hand-drawn sketches, digital art, and illustrations all work. Characters with defined body parts (head, arms, legs) get the best results from our AI pose detection. High contrast drawings with solid backgrounds are ideal.

No! Our platform is designed to be accessible to everyone. The AI handles the complex parts of animation like rigging and motion interpolation. Just upload your drawing, choose a motion, and export.

Yes! You own your original artwork and any animations created from it. Paid plans include full commercial rights to use your exported animations however you like - YouTube, social media, games, client work, etc.

Traditional animation software requires you to manually rig characters and create frame-by-frame animations. Our AI automates this process - it detects character poses automatically and applies pre-made or AI-generated motion. What might take hours in other tools can be done in minutes here.

Currently, Animate a Drawing is web-based and works on any device with a browser. A mobile app is on our roadmap for future development. The website is mobile-responsive, so you can use it on phones and tablets, though the desktop experience is currently optimal for the full editor.

Getting Started

  1. Sign up for a free account
  2. Go to Quick Animate
  3. Upload your drawing (PNG, JPG, GIF, or WebP)
  4. Wait for AI to detect the pose
  5. Choose a motion preset (walk, wave, dance, etc.)
  6. Preview and export your animation

We support PNG, JPG, GIF, and WebP images up to 10MB. PNG with transparent backgrounds works best, but any format will work. We automatically remove backgrounds for non-transparent images.

Quick Animate: Upload one drawing, apply a motion preset, export. Perfect for simple GIFs and quick content. Up to 60 seconds duration.

Full Projects: Multiple characters, scenes, backgrounds, camera movements, audio, and timeline editing. Ideal for more complex animations and stories with no time limit.

We recommend images between 500-2000 pixels on the longest side. Very small images may have poor pose detection, while very large images take longer to process. The character should take up most of the frame for best detection results.

Our platform is optimized for drawn characters and illustrations. While it may work with photos, the results may not look natural because the system is designed for 2D artwork. For best results, stick to drawings, sketches, and digital illustrations.

For best results: use a transparent or solid color background, make sure the character has visible arms and legs (not hidden behind objects), use a front-facing or 3/4 view pose, and ensure high contrast between the character and background. T-pose or arms-away-from-body poses work best for automatic detection.

Animation & Features

Our AI uses MediaPipe to automatically detect character poses and create a skeleton rig. Once rigged, you can apply motion presets or generate custom animations from text prompts. The system then interpolates between keyframes to create smooth animations.

We offer a variety of presets including: Walk, Run, Wave, Dance, Jump, Idle (breathing), Talk, Clap, Bow, and more. We're constantly adding new presets based on user feedback. You can preview any preset before applying it to your character.

Yes! You can manually adjust the rig, create custom keyframes, or describe the motion you want in text and our AI will generate it. Full projects include a timeline editor for precise animation control and custom keyframe editing.

No problem! You can manually adjust the rig using our editor. Click and drag joints to reposition them, add new joints, or connect them differently. Some art styles work better with manual rigging.

Quick Animate supports up to 60 seconds. Full projects can be much longer - short form (up to 60s), medium (up to 5 minutes), and full-length animations with multiple scenes and characters have no hard limit.

You can export as MP4 (best quality, with audio), WebM (smaller file size, web-optimized), GIF (for social media and messaging), or PNG sequence (for advanced editing in other software). Video exports support up to 4K resolution at 24, 30, or 60 FPS.

Yes! Full projects support voice synthesis (text-to-speech), automatic lip sync, background music, and sound effects. You can generate AI voices from text or upload your own audio files.

Yes, in Full Projects you can add multiple characters to each scene. Each character can have its own animation, and you can control their positioning and layering in the scene editor.

Technical

We use a combination of open source AI models: MediaPipe for pose detection, rembg (U2-Net) for background removal, custom motion generation models, and FFmpeg for video encoding. All processing happens on our servers.

Our AI processing runs on our servers in the cloud. This means you don't need a powerful computer or GPU - just upload your artwork and we handle the heavy lifting. Your browser only needs to display the results.

Pose detection typically takes 5-15 seconds depending on image size. Exporting a Quick Animate GIF usually takes 10-30 seconds. Longer videos and higher resolutions take more time. Full project exports show progress status.

We support all modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome. Some features may have limited functionality on mobile browsers.

We're working on a public API for developers. Contact us if you're interested in early access for integration with your own applications or if you have enterprise needs.

Our servers are located in the United States. All data transfers are encrypted using HTTPS. We use reliable cloud infrastructure to ensure high availability and fast processing times.
