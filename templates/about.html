Animate a Drawing is an AI-powered platform that brings your artwork to life. Upload any drawing, and our technology automatically detects poses, creates rigs, and applies animations.

Our Mission

We believe everyone should be able to create animations, regardless of technical skill. Our mission is to democratize animation by providing powerful AI tools that make the process accessible and fun.

How It Works

Our platform uses state-of-the-art open source AI models for pose detection and motion generation:

  • MediaPipe for accurate pose detection and skeletal tracking
  • rembg (U2-Net) for automatic background removal
  • Custom motion models for generating natural animations from text prompts
  • FFmpeg for high-quality video encoding

About FAQ

Animate a Drawing was created by John, a developer who wanted to make animation accessible to artists without requiring them to learn complex software.

We use a combination of open source AI models: MediaPipe for pose detection, rembg (U2-Net) for background removal, and custom motion generation models for creating smooth animations.

Our AI processing runs on our servers in the cloud. This means you don't need a powerful computer - just upload your artwork and we handle the heavy lifting.

We use MediaPipe Pose, which can detect 33 body landmarks including face, hands, and body positions. It works well with drawn characters and adapts to various art styles.

Your artwork is stored securely and only used to provide our service. We don't use your drawings to train AI models without explicit consent. See our privacy policy for full details.

Our servers are located in the United States. All data transfers are encrypted using HTTPS.
