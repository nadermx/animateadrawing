About Animate a Drawing

Making animation accessible to everyone

Our Mission

We believe everyone should be able to create animations, regardless of technical skill or expensive software.

Traditional animation requires years of practice, specialized software, and often expensive hardware. We're changing that by using AI to handle the complex parts - pose detection, rigging, and motion interpolation - so you can focus on what matters: your creativity.

Whether you're a professional artist looking to add motion to your portfolio, a content creator making engaging social media posts, an educator explaining concepts visually, or a parent wanting to bring your child's drawing to life, Animate a Drawing makes it possible.

After watching friends struggle with traditional animation tools just to create simple character movements, it became clear there had to be a better way. The explosion of AI and machine learning capabilities made it possible to automate the tedious parts of animation while keeping the creative control in the artist's hands.

What started as a side project to animate stick figures has grown into a full platform supporting professional artists, educators, game developers, and hobbyists around the world.

The Technology

Our platform is built on powerful open source AI technology, carefully integrated to provide a seamless animation experience:

MediaPipe

Google's MediaPipe provides accurate pose detection, identifying 33 body landmarks including face, hands, and body positions. It works remarkably well with drawn characters and adapts to various art styles.

rembg (U2-Net)

For automatic background removal, we use rembg powered by U2-Net. This allows characters on any background to be isolated automatically, making animation setup seamless.

Motion Generation

Our custom motion models generate natural-looking animations from simple text descriptions. Combined with a library of pre-made motion presets, you can achieve professional results quickly.

FFmpeg

For video encoding and export, we rely on FFmpeg - the industry standard for media processing. This ensures high-quality outputs in all common formats.
