Tips for Better Detection

Get the best results from our AI

Do This
  • Use clear, visible character outlines
  • Keep limbs visible (not hidden behind body)
  • Use good contrast between character and background
  • Front-facing or 3/4 view works best
  • Upload at least 500px on the shortest side
Avoid This
  • Extremely stylized poses (floating limbs)
  • Multiple overlapping characters
  • Very low resolution images
  • Characters facing completely away
  • Heavy occlusion of body parts

Manual Adjustment Option

While our AI is highly accurate, you always have the option to manually adjust the detected pose points if needed. Simply drag any landmark to fine-tune the rig before animating.

This is useful for highly stylized characters where the AI might need a little help understanding the anatomy.
